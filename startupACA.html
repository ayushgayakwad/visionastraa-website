About VisionAstraa StartUp Academy

At VisionAstraa StartUp Academy, we help college students turn their dreams into reality by guiding and mentoring them throughout their academic journey. Our goal is to support the creation of visionary companies. Startups are crucial for modern economies, driving innovation and progress.

We work with Computer Science and other engineering departments to train students from the beginning, helping them brainstorm ideas, develop business models, and register their startups. Our support includes finding customers, creating marketing plans, and building and growing teams.

Starting a company is challenging and filled with hurdles. We take pride in seeing successful companies launch and contribute to the economy and our country.

Remember, it's a long journey, not a sprint,Be ready for an exciting and extended adventure.
