Learning Outcomes

  • Master the fundamentals of electric vehicle technology and the automotive industry.

Requirements

Bachelor's degree (B.Tech) or Master's degree (M.Tech) in:

  • Electrical Engineering (EEE)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Mechanical Engineering (ME) and allied branches (Industrial, Automotive, Instrumentation, Aeronautical, etc.)
