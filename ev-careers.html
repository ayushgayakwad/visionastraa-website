Sales And Marketing Intern

Roles And Responsibilities:

  • Assist the team with lead generation and qualification activities.
  • Provide insights and implement strategies to meet company goals.
  • Craft compelling marketing materials like social media posts, email campaigns, or presentations.
  • Conduct market research and competitor analysis to inform marketing strategies.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Graduate or currently pursuing Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in business or a related field.
  • Having a sound understanding of the startup sector.
  • Must be aware of the latest business policies and market regulations.
  • Great time management as well as marketing skills.
  • Collaborative, interactive, and leadership abilities are necessary.
  • Should be a critical and analytical thinker, and solution provider.
  • Must have visionary business insight.

Who Can Apply:

  • Are available for 2 months.
  • Are ready for a remote internship.
  • Can start the internship immediately.
  • Have keen interest in sales and marketing.
  • Can work both independently and in a team.

Perks And Benefits:

  • Flexible working hours.

  • Recommendations on LinkedIn.

Role:
Technical Telecaller

We are seeking a dynamic and results-oriented individual to the role of Technical Telecaller to join our Sales & Marketing Team. As a Technical Telecaller, you will play a crucial role in generating leads, qualifying prospects, and building strong customer relationships in the EdTech segment.

Roles And Responsibilities:

  • Outbound calling to potential customers to generate leads and schedule appointments.
  • Qualifying leads based on predetermined criteria.
  • Providing accurate and informative product/service information to potential customers.
  • Addressing customer inquiries and concerns in a professional and timely manner.
  • Maintaining accurate records of customer interactions and call outcomes.
  • Achieving monthly and quarterly sales targets.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and ability to build rapport with customers.
  • Proven track record in telemarketing or sales.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience.
  • Proficient in verbal communication in multiple Indian languages is a bonus - Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Marathi.

Duration:

  • 2 months probation/internship period with performance-based incentives.
  • Full-Time with fixed monthly stipend and bonus after probation/internship period.

Perks And Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and commission structure.
  • Flexible working hours.
  • Work from Home opportunity.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.
  • Supportive and collaborative work environment.
