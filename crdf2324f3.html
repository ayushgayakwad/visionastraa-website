Test Instructions

Read the instructions carefully to ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience.

  • Test Duration: You have 15 minutes to complete the test. The timer will start as soon as you begin the test.
  • Number of Questions: The test consists of 30 multiple-choice questions (MCQs).
  • Test Access: You are allowed to take the test only once. If you exit the test before completing it, you will not be able to retake it.
  • Starting the Test: Click on the “Start Test” button to begin. Ensure you are ready to complete the test in one sitting.
  • Answering Questions: For each question, select the option that you believe is correct. You can change your answer at any time before submitting the test.
  • Submitting the Test: Once you have answered all the questions, click the “Submit" button to finalize your answers. Submitting the test will end your session.
  • Time Management: Keep an eye on the timer displayed on the screen. You will be automatically logged out once the time limit is reached.
  • Exiting the Test: If you exit the test, you will not be able to return or retake it. Ensure you have sufficient time and a stable internet connection before starting.
  • Academic Integrity: Ensure that you complete the test independently. Collaboration or cheating will not be tolerated.

Click "Start Test" to begin the test. Good Luck.
