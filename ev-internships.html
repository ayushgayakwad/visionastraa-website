    Course Content

    Branch Course Duration (in-person in RVCE) Curriculum
    ECE/EEE and allied
    • Introduction to Automative Systems: EVs, ECUs, Microcontrollers, Sensors and Actuators
    • Embedded Systems: Software Stack, RTOS, PID Controller, HMI, PWM and Motor Control
    • Communication Protocols used in EVs
    • PID Controllers, STM Automotive MCUs, STM32CubeMX, FreeRTOS, CMSIS RTOS V2
    • Interfacing Sensors & Actuators, Automotive Buses, ADAS Basics
    • Vehicle Diagnostic Tools, Collision Avoidance Systems, Gesture Controlled Car
    		 4 Weeks
    CSE/ISE and allied
    • EV fundamentals, Battery Management System, Traction Motors, Charging Infrastructure
    • Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Feature Engineering with Powertrain Optimization
    • Artificial Neural Network, Convolutional Neural Network, Kalman Filters
    • Reinforcement Learning, State, Agent, Reward, Policy and Predicitive Maintenance
    • Generative AI for EV Design, Cyber Security for EVs and Anomaly Detection
    • Applications: Thermal Predicition, Fault Diagnosis, Motor Control, Driving Patterns
    		 4 Weeks

    • Hands-on EV Component Design and Simulation
    • Thermal Management in EV Batteries, Motors and Cooling Systems
    • Kinematics and Dynamics of Suspension, Systems, Regenerative Breaking
    • Battery Pack Enclosure and Integration, Crash Safety and Performance Stress Analysis
    • EV Drivetrain Layout Design, Motor Integration, Transmission and Mounting
    • Aerodynamics, Computational Fluid Dynamics and Energy Efficiency on EV
